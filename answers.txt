maintains a local and remote backup of your repository, allows you to keep track of different

Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging and rebasing do similar functions, however, they do them in slightly 
different manners. Both commands combine changes from one branch into another. Merge
does this by connecting the two branches and maintaining the histories of each individually.
Rebasing, on the other hand, places on entire branch on top of another and makes them one, linear
branch.
